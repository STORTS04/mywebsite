Cockatiels - The Charming Companions

What Are Cockatiels?

Cockatiels are small, friendly parrots native to Australia. They are known for their beautiful crest, social nature, and whistling ability. They make excellent pets for both beginners and experienced bird owners.

Appearance

Cockatiels typically have grey feathers, a yellow face, and orange cheek patches. There are also other color mutations such as lutino (white and yellow), pied, and albino.

Care Tips

  • Provide a spacious cage and allow regular out-of-cage time.
  • Feed a balanced diet of seeds, pellets, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Clean the cage regularly and ensure fresh water is always available.
  • Socialize with your cockatiel daily—they love attention!

Fun Facts

  • Cockatiels can mimic whistles and even simple tunes.
  • They express emotion with their crest—upright means alert or excited.
  • They can live up to 20 years with proper care.
